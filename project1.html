Video Wall Setup

Project by Connor Bescos

Project Description

The objective of this project is to create a video wall that emulates a realistic and immersive window display, composed of three adjacent TVs. The video wall will be installed in a designated area and will be used primarily for displaying various nature or landscape scenes, such as mountains, beaches, and forests, to provide a serene and calming environment.

The video wall will be controlled by a computer that can switch between different video sources, allowing for dynamic and varied content. The installation will be done with high-quality mounting brackets, and the TVs will be calibrated to match color and brightness. Finally, the overall design of the video wall will aim to provide a seamless and integrated experience.
